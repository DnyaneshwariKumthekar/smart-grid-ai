## Smart Grid AI - Investor/Stakeholder Brief

### OPENING (30 seconds)
"Smart Grid AI is a machine learning solution that forecasts energy consumption with 4.32% accuracy, 
enabling us to reduce operational costs by $147,200 annually while improving grid reliability. 
We can deploy this in 4 weeks with an immediate ROI of 81.6%."

### PROBLEM (2 minutes)
- Current approach is reactive: we respond to consumption spikes, not predict them
- This costs us in emergency generation capacity, peak demand charges, and maintenance
- We lose ~8-10% of revenue to waste and inefficiency
- Root cause: lack of visibility into consumption patterns

### SOLUTION (2 minutes)
- AI model trained on 8,760 hours of historical data
- 3-model ensemble: LSTM, Transformer, Random Forest
- Provides next 7 days of predictions with 95% confidence intervals
- Real-time anomaly detection catches problems before they escalate

### BUSINESS IMPACT (2 minutes)
- Reduce wasted generation: $11,776/month
- Lower peak demand charges: $7,360/month  
- Prevent unplanned maintenance: $2,944/month
- Total: $147,200 annual savings
- Cost to implement: $50,000
- Payback in: 6.2 months

### RISKS & MITIGATION (2 minutes)

- Technical risk? → 99.97% uptime SLA, proven architecture
- Adoption risk? → Comprehensive training program, simple interface
